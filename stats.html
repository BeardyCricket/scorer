What are Win Zones?

Win Zones shows which type of pair innings tends to win matches — and how often your pair plays that way. Every pair innings (you + your partner for that match) is placed in one of 9 zones based on two things: how many wickets your pair lost, and how many boundaries (4s and 6s) your pair hit.

How to read it: The large coloured percentage is the overall win rate for all pairs who landed in that zone. Below it is the total number of pair innings across all players. "Your pair" shows how many of your pair innings fell there — green background means your pair won at least once from that zone 🏆.

Colours: Green = 40%+ win rate · Amber = 15–39% · Red = under 15%

Use it to understand what style of innings gives you the best chance of winning — and whether your natural game puts you in the high-win zones.
