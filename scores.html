Pairs formatPlayers split into pairs who bat together for a set number of overs. Every pair bats and bowls.
🏏
BattingRuns are scored normally. The batting pair accumulates runs across all their overs.
❌
Wickets cost 5 runsEach wicket deducts 5 runs from the batting pair's total. Batters keep going — no one leaves the field.
⭐
Bowling bonusBowlers earn +2 bonus points added to their pair's total for every wicket they take.
🏆
WinningThe pair with the highest total — batting runs plus bowling bonus — wins.
